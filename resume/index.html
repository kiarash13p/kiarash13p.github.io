Interests and Goals



Improve my thinking and interpesonal skills Tap for more

From my perspective, every question contains half of its answer. People usually focus only on finding results without considering the definition and demands of the question. My major interest is to facilitate the procedure of finding robust and sustainable ways of thinking about a question and having a contribution to innovative solutions for future generations, with individuals and groups. This is not only precious but a vital movement in a world full of unforeseen and complicated questions and illusions. I would like to be engaged in activities in which I can positively teach and learn my knowledge and expertises.

Pursue my education Tap for more

The nature of science demands regular purification and should be respected and treated intensely. However, the current education system has been limited to degrees; such purification can be achieved through continuous learning and teaching. New subjects, new solving approaches, new answers, and consequently, new demands have always been raised. Most traditional beliefs work poorly, and a more comprehensive lookout is required. Thanks to technological advancements, I would like to implement them in my efforts in meaningful and reasonable ways by being educated and supervised by like-minded experts and professionals.

Supply Value Chain of Critical Minerals Tap for more

Modern economies rely on countless cohorts of raw materials, from iron and limestone to gold and semiconductors. Minerals have mostly been traded in sustained and organized markets, but, conversely, critical minerals such as lithium do not have this opportunity yet. My main interest is to leverage my wisdom and expertise in enlightening critical minerals supply chain (CMSC) transparency, responsibility, and resilience. Thus, it is required to carry out R&D to solve the challenges of CRMSC.

Teaching and Democratizing AI Tap for more

Artificial Intelligence bears a heavy burden of human activities in the contemporary world, and modern societies need to teach and explain AI to new generations clearly to preserve the chain of innovation and technology improvement. Thus, one of my interests is to combine mine and experts wisdom and experiences to facilitate sustainable education of AI and its development.
