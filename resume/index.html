Interests



Improve thinking and innovation

From my perspective, every question contains half of its answer. People usually focus only on finding results without considering the definition and demands of the question. My major interest is to facilitate the procedure of finding robust and sustainable ways of thinking about a question and having a contribution to innovative solutions for future generations. This is not a precious but a vital movement in a world full of unforeseen and complicated questions and illusions.

Pursue my education

The nature of science demands regular purification and should be respected and treated intensely. Although the current education system has been limited to degrees, such purification can be done by continuous learning and teaching achievements. New subjects, new solving approaches, new answers, and consequently, new demands have always been raised. Most traditional beliefs work poorly, and a more comprehensive lookout is required.

Teaching and Democratizing AI

Artificial Intelligence bears a heavy burden of human activities in the contemporary world, and modern societies need to teach and explain AI to new generations clearly to preserve the chain of innovation and technology improvement. Thus, one of my interests is to combine mine and experts wisdom and experiences to facilitate sustainable education of AI and its development.

Supply Value Chain of Critical Minerals

Modern economies rely on countless cohorts of raw materials, from iron and limestone to gold and semiconductors. Mostly, minerals have been traded on sustained and organized markets, but conversely, critical minerals such as Lithium do not have this opportunity yet. My main interest is to leverage my wisdom and expertise in enlightening critical minerals supply chain (CMSC) transparency, responsiblity, and resilience. Thus, it is required to carry out R&D to solve the challenges of CMSC.
